Repurpo-phone

Repurpo-phone is an organization that turns your old or broken phones into new products such as security cameras or music players. This organization aims to reduce the amount of old or broken phones that go into land fills or go unused in people's homes. Not only that, cellphones contain harmful substances such as lead, which can harm the environment if left to rot in land fills. Lastly, repurposing your old or broken phones can reduce energy consumption when creating a new one.
